Welcome to

CEEIIC STUDENT NETWORK

The CEEIIC Student Network coordinates and strengthens student-led programs within the CEEIIC ecosystem.

About Us Explore Programs

Empowering Student Innovation & Leadership

A formalised coordination and collaboration platform established to strengthen and align student-led programmes operating under the CEEIIC ecosystem.

We break down silos and cultivate cross-programme collaboration through coordinated planning, joint events, and shared learning spaces.

The Network enables students to openly collaborate, share resources, exchange skills, and support one another's initiatives, creating an enabling environment for collective growth and ecosystem-wide success.

Value Proposition

Strategic Impact

The CEEIIC-SN adds value at three levels

Institutional

  • Strengthens student-centeredness
  • Advances research & innovation
  • Supports people & environment
  • Enhances internationalization

Directorate

  • Reduces duplication & silos
  • Centralises reporting
  • Improves succession planning
  • Strengthens institutional memory

Student

  • Access to diverse opportunities
  • Leadership development
  • Peer learning & mentorship
  • Increased employability

Target Beneficiaries

Undergraduate and postgraduate students across all faculties, including student representatives (SRC).

Program Attributes:

  • Research-based learning & problem-solving
  • Diversity & equitable access
  • Community development & social impact
  • Practical & leadership skills
  • Enterprise development & innovation

Governance Structure

The governance structure of the network shall comprise of support staff and the executive leaders of the respective programmes within the network.

All programs submit Quarterly and Yearly reports
Performance analyzed annually by coordinators and unit managers
