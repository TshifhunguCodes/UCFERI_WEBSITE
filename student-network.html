ABOUT CEEIIC STUDENT NETWORK

Empowering Student Innovation & Leadership

The CEEIIC Student Network is a formalised coordination and collaboration platform established to strengthen and align student-led programmes operating under the CEEIIC ecosystem.

10+ Student Programs
Cross-Programme Collaboration
Shared Resources & Skills
Institutional Impact

We break down silos and cultivate cross-programme collaboration through coordinated planning, joint events, and shared learning spaces. The Network enables students to openly collaborate, share resources, exchange skills, and support one another's initiatives, creating an enabling environment for collective growth and ecosystem-wide success.

Aligned with Univen's Strategic Thrusts: Student Centeredness • Research & Innovation • People & Environment • Internationalisation

Value Proposition

Strategic Impact

The CEEIIC-SN adds value at three levels

Institutional

  • ✓ Strengthens student-centeredness (ST1)
  • ✓ Advances research, innovation, entrepreneurship (ST2)
  • ✓ Supports people, health, environment (ST3)
  • ✓ Enhances internationalization & partnerships (ST4)

Directorate

  • ✓ Reduces duplication and programme silos
  • ✓ Centralises reporting and impact tracking
  • ✓ Improves succession planning
  • ✓ Strengthens institutional memory

Student

  • ✓ Access to diverse opportunities
  • ✓ Leadership development pathways
  • ✓ Peer-to-peer learning and mentorship
  • ✓ Increased visibility and employability

Target Beneficiaries

Undergraduate and postgraduate students across all faculties, including student representatives (SRC).

Program Attributes:

  • ✓ Programs that promote research-based learning, problem-solving, and interdisciplinary collaboration
  • ✓ Programs that promote participation, diversity, and equitable access to opportunities
  • ✓ Programs that focus on community development, social entrepreneurship, and societal impact
  • ✓ Programs that equip students with practical, professional, and leadership skills
  • ✓ Programs that directly support enterprise development, business creation, and innovation

Join the network

Any Univen student can join

Undergraduate and postgraduate students across all faculties. Participate in any programme of your choice and gain access to leadership pathways, mentorship, and opportunities.

Recruitment Process:

  • Programs incepted within the directorate or spin-offs
  • Alignment with shared values
  • Submit program overview, ToR, log frame for assessment
  • No political programs, parties, or societies
  • Active participation = recognition

Structure

Governance & Oversight

Governance Structure

The governance structure of the network shall comprise of support staff and the executive leaders of the respective programmes within the network.

  • All programs submit Quarterly and Yearly reports
  • Performance analyzed annually by coordinators and unit managers
  • Program Coordinators from each unit
  • Executives of each program

Sustainability

The network will sustain its operations through:

  • Partnerships
  • Fundraising activities
  • Sponsorships
  • Grants
