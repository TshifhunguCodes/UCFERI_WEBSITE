University Of Venda Centre For Entrepreneurship And Rapid Incubation (UCfERI) serves as a dynamic hub that nurtures and cultivates entrepreneurs, fostering their growth and innovation to propel the greater rural economy of the Vhembe district. We provide integrated entrepreneurial development support and creative interventions to startups.
