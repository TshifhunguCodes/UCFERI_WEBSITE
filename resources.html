Students across all faculties with entrepreneurial aspirations, supported through ESAP and other student programs.

Youth (18-35 years)

Young people in Vhembe region seeking entrepreneurial opportunities and skills development.

SFFP Skills Training Mentorship

Women Entrepreneurs

Female entrepreneurs and aspiring business owners, with special focus through SWEEP program.
