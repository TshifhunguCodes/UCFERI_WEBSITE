Pop-Up Market

UCfERI Elim Pop-Up Market: Empowering Local Entrepreneurs

2026 Elim Mall, Limpopo Makhado Municipality | SEDFA | Twin City Group

UCfERI Elim Pop-Up Market in Collaboration with Makhado Municipality, SEDFA and Twin City Group (Elim Mall).

The University of Venda Centre for Entrepreneurship Rapid Incubation (UCfERI), in collaboration with Makhado municipality, @sedfa_dsbd and Twin City Group (Elim Mall), hosted the Elim Pop-Up Market, creating a vibrant platform for local entrepreneurs to showcase their products, connect with customers, and grow their businesses.

This initiative reflects a shared commitment to fostering economic growth, supporting small businesses, and creating sustainable livelihoods for entrepreneurs in the Vhembe district.

Keynote Address

Honourable Deputy Minister Dipuo Peters on Economic Growth & Youth Employment

November 14, 2023 University of Venda UCFERI Launch Event

A special thank you to Honourable Deputy Minister Ms. Dipuo Peters for gracing us with a Keynote address that resonated deeply. Her insights touched on crucial points that truly align with our strategy - from growing our local economy to tackling the high unemployment rate.

The Deputy Minister shared a powerful vision where our graduates become not just job seekers, but well-rounded contributors to society. Her words inspired all in attendance and reinforced the mission of the University of Venda Centre for Entrepreneurship and Rapid Incubation.

The address formed a highlight of the memorable UCFERI Launch, setting the tone for a future of innovation, collaboration, and empowerment for the next generation of entrepreneurs and small business owners in our community.
