UCFERI partners with UCBAC for business support outreach

In a collaborative effort to uplift emerging enterprises, the Centre for Entrepreneurship and Rapid Incubation (UCFERI) partnered with the University of Venda Community Business Advisory Centre (UCBAC) to conduct a targeted business support outreach at Makhado Crossing Mall in Louis Trichardt on 31 July 2025. The initiative formed part of UNIVEN's broader strategy to foster inclusive economic development.
