Our Mission & Vision

Vision: To be a leading centre for entrepreneurial excellence where student entrepreneurs and SMMEs thrive.

Mission: To accelerate the growth of SMMEs by delivering comprehensive support programmes and fostering strong internal and external networks while creating an inclusive, enabling environment that unlocks the potential of staff, student and community entrepreneurs as drivers of sustainable socio-economic development.

The UNIVEN Centre for Entrepreneurship and Rapid Incubation (UCfERI) is a division within the Directorate of Community Engagement, Entrepreneurship, Inclusive Innovation and Commercialisation (CEEIIC). Established in 2023, UCfERI provides creative and innovative students, startups and SMMEs with facilities and integrated entrepreneurial development support needed to launch and grow sustainable businesses.

Our Journey

Milestones strengthening entrepreneurship ecosystem

2023

Establishment of UCfERI

Centre formally established under the Directorate of Community Engagement, Entrepreneurship, Inclusive Innovation and Commercialisation, aligning with UNIVEN Strategic Thrust 2.

2024

Advisory Board Inauguration

Inaugural advisory board formed with industry leaders including Sedfa representative and board chairperson Mrs. Hwalani Mabaso.

2024

SEDFA 18-Month Service Flow Adoption

UCfERI officially adopts the Sedfa 18-months service flow model to support students, student enterprises and community entrepreneurs.

2025

ISO & Quality Management Training Launch

Rollout of ISO 22000, ISO 9000 and ISO 14000 standards training for SMMEs, enhancing competitiveness.

2025

Expansion of Market Access & IP Support

Full suite operational: legal/admin support, product testing, IP registration, business accreditation, and trade exhibitions.
