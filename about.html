Our Mission & Vision

Vision: To be a leading centre for entrepreneurial excellence where student entrepreneurs and SMMEs thrive.

Mission: To accelerate the growth of SMMEs by delivering comprehensive support programmes and fostering strong internal and external networks while creating an inclusive, enabling environment that unlocks the potential of staff, student and community entrepreneurs as drivers of sustainable socio-economic development.

UCfERI (University of Venda Centre for Entrepreneurship and Rapid Incubation) was established in November 2023 and commenced operations in July 2024. We are located within the UNESCO Vhembe Biosphere Reserve, offering unique opportunities for agro-processing, agro-tourism, and sustainable development.
