Program Structure

6-month intensive program with 3 phases

01

Foundation Phase
(Months 1-2)

  • Business idea validation
  • Market research training
  • Basic financial literacy
  • Legal requirements workshop
02

Development Phase
(Months 3-4)

  • Business plan development
  • Marketing strategy creation
  • Financial projections
  • Pitch deck preparation
03

Launch Phase
(Months 5-6)

  • Minimum viable product launch
  • Market entry strategies
  • Investor pitch preparation
  • Post-launch support planning

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the program

Is there any cost to join the incubation program?

The program is fully funded for selected participants. There are no costs for training, mentorship, or basic resources provided by UCfERI.

What kind of businesses are accepted?

We accept businesses in various sectors including agriculture, technology, services, manufacturing, and retail. The business should have potential for growth and job creation in Vhembe District.

What support is provided after the 6-month program?

Graduates receive 6 months of post-incubation support including continued mentorship, networking opportunities, and access to funding information.

Can I apply if I'm still employed?

Yes, but you must demonstrate commitment to attending all required sessions and developing your business alongside your employment.
